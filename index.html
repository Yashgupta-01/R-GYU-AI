AI Courtroom Debate Simulator Guide

How it Works

  1. Provide your Gemini API Key using the settings button in the top right.
  2. Choose a topic manually or click the dice button 🎲 to pick a random debate topic.
  3. Select your Litigants (the Plaintiff and Defendant archetypes). Each has a unique ideological persona and memory capacity.
  4. Convene the courtroom to start the live debate!
  5. Inject Coach Notes: While a debater is waiting or actively speaking, you can click "Whisper Instruction" to guide their next arguments privately.
  6. Judicial Review: After both sides finish speaking in a round, Justice AI scores the arguments on logic, evidence, and persuasiveness.
  7. Audit Fallacies: Once all rounds finish, run the Fallacy Detector overlay to scan for logic gaps and download the final record.
